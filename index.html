The Protea Flower

Proteas are a unique and iconic symbol of and national flower of South Africa, representing the country's rich and diverse flora

The protea flower needs fire to flourish. Proteas, which are a type of fynbos, are referred to as a pyrophylic or fire-loving vegetation, and in need to burn every few years to survive.

The king protea flower is large when opened and can grow to about 12 inches in diameter. Flower petals are stiff and tapered and are often light-colored on the bottom and then become pink or red nearer to the top.
